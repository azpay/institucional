Our goal is to make online payments more easy, secure and transparent without the traditional hassle and complex coding

We started innovating the online payment world in 2013. It was a bumpy road, with a just a few members on the team. Since day one we intended to make ecommerces sell more with a lower transaction fee and less of a hassle to integrate our solution.

Convincing businesses to migrate their online payment solution to AZPay was not easy.

But we had a strong factor: saving money with less complexity to the business owner, their financial team and also the development team. We never intended to ease the life of just one of them, that was too easy.

Using any of our products, businesses can take advantage of one of the best payment solutions in the internet. Everything was built to their customers needs, because if their business is happy, mission acomplished.
